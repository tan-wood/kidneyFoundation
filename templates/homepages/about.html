What is KidneyWatch?

In the US, 1 in 7 adults are estimated to have Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

That means that life for apporximately 37 million Americans includes high blood pressure, heart disease, and stroke caused by CKD

Still, there is hope

Studies have shown nutrition plays a large part in the health of those battling CKD

The DASH diet in particular has been found to decrease the risk of stroke, cancer, kidney stones, and high blood pressure

KidneyWatch helps users impliment the DASH diet into their life with tools such as personalized meal suggestions and real-time notifications

{%if "High Blood Pressure" in conditions and "Diabetes" not in conditions %}

The DASH diet was originally designed to help people with high blood pressure. The diet revolves around greens, fruits, and lean proteins. The avoidance of fats, sodium, and red meats makes adherence to the DASH diet a key factor in fighting against high blood pressure.

{% elif "Diabetes" in conditions and "High Blood Pressure" not in conditions %}

The DASH diet is not only limited to those with kidney disease Those with diabetes in particular have experienced improved insulin resistance, hyperlipidemia, and lower levels of obesity when following DASH diet guidelines.
